Expert in public relations and corporate communications with a distinguished career in sports marketing and investment. Participated in developing and managing pioneering projects including Jabal Al-Qara Project (Land of Civilizations) and Al-Shaibani Park (Al-Mashqar Tourism Project). Currently serves as a Board Member of Sanad Social Responsibility Association and manages PR & Corporate Communications at the association.
